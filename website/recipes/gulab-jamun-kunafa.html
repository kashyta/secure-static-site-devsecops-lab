Gulab Jamun Kunafa

← Back to Home

Overview

A delightful fusion of Middle Eastern and Indian desserts — crispy kunafa nests filled with unsweetened cream and mini Gulab Jamuns, topped with pistachios and drizzled with aromatic sugar syrup. A perfect balance of textures and sweetness.

Ingredients

Kunafa Nests

  • 250g Kunafa dough
  • 1/2 cup melted butter

Gulab Jamun

  • 180g Gulab Jamun mix

Filling & Garnish

  • 150g fresh cream (unsweetened)
  • Pistachios, chopped (for garnish)

Sugar Syrup

  • 3 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 5 cardamom pods
  • 1 tsp saffron

Instructions

Prepare Sugar Syrup

  1. Combine sugar and water in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir until dissolved and bring to a boil.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in lemon juice, cardamom, and saffron.
  3. Pour 3/4 of the syrup into a wide dish for soaking Gulab Jamuns. Reserve the rest for drizzling over Kunafa nests.

Make Gulab Jamuns

  1. Prepare dough as per mix instructions.
  2. Form small balls (1–2 cm diameter) and deep fry until golden brown and cooked through.
  3. Soak in prepared sugar syrup for at least 45 minutes.

Prepare Kunafa Nests

  1. Grease a cupcake pan and preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Brush Kunafa dough strips with melted butter.
  3. Twist and shape into nests, pressing into cupcake molds.
  4. Bake for 12–15 minutes until crisp and golden. Let cool.

Assemble Dessert

  1. Place Kunafa nests on serving plate.
  2. Spread a layer of fresh cream inside each nest.
  3. Add 2–3 mini Gulab Jamuns on top.
  4. Drizzle reserved sugar syrup over each nest.
  5. Garnish with chopped pistachios and serve.
